Amigurumi

Making different amigurumi is always fun. I love creating different cute creatures and giving them life; it is especially interesting since even if I may be crochet the same thing, every creation has little ways in which it differs from the rest.

They’re all unique and they’re own characters. Amigurumi is definitely one of my favorite hobbies!

Cactuses

These small, friendly plants can be the perfect addition to your apartment or office space’s atmosphere!

Without the necessity to worry over maintaining the cactus’ health, they are the fun and ideal gift that your friends or family members never knew they needed. The needleless cactus doesn’t requires sun nor water, only the permission to spruce up your home.
