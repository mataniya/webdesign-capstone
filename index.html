Welcome

Welcome to my Crafty Corner! I organized my crafting throughout the last couple years to share on this site. Mostly I like to spend my free time handcrafting. After a busy day, it is very relaxing to knit, crochet, etc. This is not only an excellent way to relieve stress, but also you can create useful or fun things. This website is a collection of some of my works. Maybe it can inspire some of you to try to make some crafts of your own! I hope that you enjoy my site and my creations!

About me

Hi, my name is Tanya. I have an M.S. in Applied Math, and I love to learn new things. I also like books, music, and creating. I love to knit, crochet (especially amigurumi) and have a large supply of yarn and patterns that I want to make. But first and foremost I am the mother of two wonderful children and a wife. I used to spend a large portion of my time on my family, yet now that my children have gotten older, I have more time for taking Coursera classes and handcrafting.
